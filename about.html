Authentic Italian Food

Family-Owned

Piccola Trattoria is an authentic Italian restaurant, family-owned and operated since 1994.
Piccola Trattoria’s seasonal menu features an array of homemade pastas such as cannelloni, gnocchi and pappardelle. Come warm up with melt-in-your-mouth Osso Buco, Boulliobese di Pesce or Branzino.
Piccola Trattoria’s cozy atmosphere is perfect for date night or to relax with good friends and a glass of vino.
